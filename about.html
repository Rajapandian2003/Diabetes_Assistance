About Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects the way your body processes blood sugar (glucose). Learn how to manage diabetes and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

There are different types of diabetes, but the most common ones are Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes.

Proper management, including a balanced diet, regular physical activity, and monitoring blood sugar levels, can help you live a healthier life with diabetes.

Diabetes Prevention

Maintain a healthy weight : Losing weight can improve how your body uses insulin and glucose. The American Diabetes Association recommends losing at least 7â€“10% of your body weight to prevent diabetes progression.

Stay active : Try to get at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise each day. You can try taking a walk, playing a sport, or doing an online exercise class.

Eat healthy : Avoid sugar and saturated fat, and try to eat healthier fats like unsalted nuts, seeds, avocados, olive oil, rapeseed oil, and sunflower oil.

Quit smoking : Smoking tobacco can increase your risk of developing diabetes.
